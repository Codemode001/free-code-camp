Introduction

Hindi ko alam kung may magbabasa ba ng documentaion na ito but let me share my life experiences sayo kung sino kaman. My life is not that interesting so if you don't want to waste time I advise you to stop reading this page right now.

Hello! my name is Harold, I'm 19 years old, a college student who strive to become a full stack developer just like my life mentor. dati paman ay mahilig na ako sa computer, specifically sa mga online games. Why? kasi hindi katulad ng ibang mga bata ay hindi ako nabiyayaan ng malakas na katawan, tangkad, at talino. I'm an anime fan and an instrumentalist. And the most important of all I am saved by grace.

Before the encounter

Before I encountered God I was so lost, to the point where i don't even know kung ano ang makakapag pasaya sa akin, I don't even know kung masaya ba ako or hindi. There's a constant emptiness na paligi kog nararamdaman where i know na merong kulang pero hindi ko alam kung saan mag sisimulang hanapin ang bagay na buboo sa kulang kong pagkatao and the worst part is that I don't even know what I'm looking for.

I keep doing the things that my friends are doing kasi sabi nila "masaya". pinalibutan ko ang sarili ko ng mga bagay na labeled ng mundo as happiness. sigarilyo, alak, women, and other worldly entertainment, i keep doing and looking for different things na makakapag satisfy ng long ko for contintment. I've been imprisoned and chained by sexual sins. You can describe me in so many different words but pure is not one of them. I keep jumping from relationship to relationship hoping na someday I will find someone who can love me for who I am. Hindi ko alam kung bakit uhaw na uhaw ako sa pag-ibig, siguro dahil napakahirap kung maramdaman ito kahit sa sarili kung pamilya I can't blame them because they are right, who would love someone like me. I'm not doing well in school lalo na sa bahay bilang lang ang mga bagay kung saan magaling ako for example magaling maging pabigat sa pamilya ko at maging sakit sa ulo.

The encounter

After a very long years of my pabadlong stage I slowly started changing my self, may mga pinakilalang mga tao ang Dios sa akin na naging mga kaibigan ko at first hindi ako makapaniwala na naging kaibigan ko sila kasi lahat sila ay mga matatalinong tao or mga honor students. The influence na mararamdaman mo sa group nayun is very strong sabi nga nila tell me who your friends are and I'll tell you who you are at ayun dati sila lang ang honor students pero dumating ang panahon na lahat kami ay hindi na honor students buwahahaha. I met this circle of friends back when I was in third year high school the same year na nakilala ko ang babaeng ginamit ni Lord to change my whole life.

At first I don't really care for this girl, but nagtaka ako, na curious ako kasi she's so different, different from all the girls I've met. She's always rebuking me whenever nagsasalita ako ng mga masasmang salita and at that time puro curse words lang talaga ang laman ng bibig ko everytime na masaya curse, everytime na malungkot curse, everytime na nagagalit curse, hindi kompleto ang araw ko ng hindi ako nakakapag curse haha. But this girl has the nerve to rebuke me everytime, as if she has the authority to tell me that what I'm doing is wrong. I grew a lot of interest from this girl because ngayun ko lang naramdaman na parang need kong sumunod sa mga sinasabi nya. She's really different, so different sa mga ugali na na encounter ko. As time goes by sya nalang ang laman ng isip ko questions are floating in my mind like how is she different? bakit iba ang atmosphere na nararamdaman ko pag nasapaligid ko sya, bakit parang whenever na kasama ko sya I feel the need to be formal, honest, and good.

Well katulad ng ibang relationships hindi din kami nag tagal, umabot sa punto where I felt na napakalayo nya sa kung nasaan ako It's like she's in a different world that I don't belong. But one thing that I've noticed is that after naming mag decide to go separate paths I did not see a single frown nor tears in her face. And boom another set of questions pops into my mind, bakit masaya sya kahit bago lang kami nagka hiwalay? siguro she did not love me, siguro side nya lang ako, siguro I'm just another guy na pampalipas oras nya ang, siguro may ibang nagpapasaya sa kanya. And I was right may iba nga nag papangiti sa kanyang mga labi sa lahat ng oras at ocasion at yun ay ang Panginoon.

The beggining of a new start

In that moment I understood na hindi ko kayang makamit ang happiness na hinahanap ko gamit ang sarili kong lakas, I understood na kaya pala masaya sya because she has something na wala ako that's the faith na meron sya sa Panginoon! And I asked God, "Lord paano ko po makakamit ang kasayahan na nasa kanya? na kahit sa mga mahihirap na sitwasyon ay kaya nyang maging masaya. I just asked God and then kinabukasan ay may pinadala ang Dios tao para matulungan ako, that's how powerful God is kaya nyang gawin ang mga bagay para sayo bastat ikakabuti mo. I met my old friend na dati kong kasama sa mga kalokohan but I was so surprised kasi nakikita ko sa kanya ang nakikita kong liwanag sa babaeng pinakilala ng Panginoon. Back then, I knew na ito na ang simula.

The Struggle

After that start, never did I thought na hindi pala magiging madali ang buhay na may kinikilalang Panginoon. Maraming mga paghihirap at sakit ang dumarating, minsan naiisip ko na mas masakit palang mag lingkod sa Panginoon kaysa sa buhay ko noon. Pero the only difference is that I know na palaging nandyan ang Panginoon para sa akin, I don't mind these problems kasi alam kong there's hope! There's light! There's a way! God will make a way!
