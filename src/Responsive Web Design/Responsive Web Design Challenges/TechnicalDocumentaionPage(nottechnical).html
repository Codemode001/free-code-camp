Introduction

Hindi ko alam kung may magbabasa ba ng documentaion na ito but let me share my life experiences sayo kung sino kaman. My life is not that interesting so if you don't want to waste time I advise you to stop reading this page right now.

Hello! my name is Harold, I'm 19 years old, a college student who strive to become a full stack developer just like my life mentor. dati paman ay mahilig na ako sa computer, specifically sa mga online games. Why? kasi hindi katulad ng ibang mga bata ay hindi ako nabiyayaan ng malakas na katawan, tangkad, at talino. I'm an anime fan and an instrumentalist. And the most important of all I am saved by grace.
